# DBT Skills Toolkit
https://dbtskills.pages.dev
A DBT (Dialectical Behavior Therapy) skills reference app built as a Progressive Web App (PWA). Dark mode, no account required, no data stored externally.

---

## What's Inside

| Module | Skills |
|--------|--------|
| 🔥 Distress Tolerance | ACCEPTS, TIPP, Radical Acceptance, Self-Soothe, Pros & Cons + Urge Surfing tool |
| 🤝 Interpersonal Effectiveness | DEAR MAN, GIVE, FAST, Validation + Script builders with export |
| 💜 Emotional Regulation | Check the Facts, Opposite Action, PLEASE Checker, Build Positive Experiences, Build Mastery + Emotion Check-In with Feelings Wheel |
| 🍃 Mindfulness | Observe, Describe, Participate, Non-Judgmentally, One-Mindfully + Guided Breathing + Wise Mind |

---

## Adding to Your Home Screen

No download or account needed — just open the link and follow the steps for your phone.

### iPhone
1. Open the link in **Safari** *(must be Safari, not Chrome)*
2. Tap the **Share button** (↑) at the bottom of the screen
3. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **"Add"**

The app will appear on your home screen and open full-screen, just like a native app.

### Android
1. Open the link in **Chrome**
2. Tap the **three-dot menu** (⋮) in the top-right corner
3. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"** *(may appear as "Install app" on newer Android)*
4. Tap **"Add"**

The app will appear on your home screen and open full-screen.

## Privacy

- No database — nothing is stored anywhere
- No accounts — no login required
- No analytics — no tracking of any kind
- All interactive tools are in-memory only and reset when the app is closed
- Exported files (.txt) save locally to your device only
